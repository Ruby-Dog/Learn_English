# 雖然
#### 1. 
```
Although  S + V ...., S + V .....
```
```
Though    S + V ...., S + V .....
```
#### 2.
```
Adj.
Adv.   as   S + V ... , S + V ....      (倒裝)
  N.
```

## 例句1
```
Although she is a girl , she is very courageous in the face of the danger.
```
```
Girl as she is  , she is very courageous in the face of the danger.
```
